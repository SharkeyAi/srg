Campus Strategy

Four separate buildings, four separate entrances, one unified SRG brand ecosystem. Guests, students, corporate staff, and logistics operations each have their own dedicated space — eliminating overlap while maximizing synergy. The Whitehall campus sits in Atlanta's HBCU corridor near I-20, serving a built-in market of 100K+ students while activating underutilized commercial property in an underserved community.
