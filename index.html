Lease Purchase Bond Program

SRG is pursuing Invest Atlanta's Lease Purchase Bond Program — the City of Atlanta purchases the Whitehall Assemblage and leases it back to SRG under long-term terms. Provides full financial runway to activate all 4 buildings without immediate real estate burden. Aligned with City goals of reactivating underutilized commercial property in the HBCU corridor. Mayor's Office has expressed early support.
